The platform

Four capabilities. One routing fabric.

Intelligence is the resource. Matrix is how work finds it — and how organizations stay in control of it.

01

AI compute routing

Send each task to the model, agent, or toolchain best suited to it. Idle capacity across your accounts and machines becomes one routable pool — dispatched in parallel, never colliding on the same work.

02

Human intelligence routing

When work needs a person — judgment, approval, expertise — Matrix routes it to the right one, with full context attached. Humans are first-class nodes on the same fabric, not an afterthought.

03

Context- & session-independent delivery

Work units carry their own context; nothing is trapped in a chat session. Hand off, resume, and audit any piece of work — anywhere, anytime, on any machine. Every record accumulates: your organization's AI work becomes an asset, not exhaust.

04

Enterprise & team governance

Permissions, review gates, write-scope locks, audit trails, and execution ledgers for every AI and human action. The wall between "AI did something" and "we can prove what happened."

How it works

Route → Govern → Deliver

Every unit of work moves through the same three-stage fabric — whoever, or whatever, does it.

Route

Work arrives as self-contained units with declared scope. Matrix matches each one to the best available intelligence — a model, an agent fleet, a toolchain, or a person — and dispatches in parallel where scopes don't overlap.

Govern

Every dispatch passes the governance wall: permissions checked, write scopes locked so no two workers fight over the same files, review gates enforced where you demand a human in the loop. Nothing runs that policy didn't allow.

Deliver

Results return as auditable records — context attached, ledger written. Hand the work to the next intelligence, resume it next week, or ship it. Delivery is independent of any session, machine, or vendor.
