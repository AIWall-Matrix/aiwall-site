Get started

Put your first work on the fabric.

Matrix onboarding is a guided join flow: it signs you in, connects your machine, and makes your capacity routable — a few minutes, end to end.

Three steps in, fully governed.

Whether you're joining a team or setting up your own fleet, the flow is the same.

  1. Open the Matrix platform entry. The join flow runs in your browser — sign in, or accept the invite your team sent you.
  2. Connect your machine. The guided install sets up the Matrix client and links it to your account — no manual configuration.
  3. Route work. Your capacity joins the fabric. Dispatch your first work unit, watch it route, and read the delivered record.
