Join your team · 加入团队

Three steps. About three minutes.

Your team runs on Matrix. Joining connects your AI work to the team board and lets your idle AI compute help the pool — credentials never leave your machine. 团队在用 Matrix。加入 = 你的 AI 使用情况接入团队看板 + 空闲 AI 算力可被团队调度。账号凭证只留在你自己的电脑上。

1

Run the installer

Paste one line into your terminal. No admin rights. When it finishes, your browser opens by itself. 终端粘贴一行命令，不需要管理员权限；装完浏览器会自动打开。

macos / linux 
curl -fsSL https://aiwall.com/downloads/matrix/stable/install.sh | sh
windows powershell 
irm https://aiwall.com/downloads/matrix/stable/matrix.ps1 | iex; matrix-install -Token <安装码> -Cloud https://join.aiwall.com

Prefer clicking to typing? Download the standalone zip and double-click 安装Matrix.command instead.

2

Paste your install code

In the browser page that just opened: sign in (or register — work email + password, ten seconds). If your PM sent you an invite link, open it to land in the team. Then copy the install code the page shows you — or use the one your PM sent, if it's fresh — and paste it back into the install window. 在打开的网页里注册/登录（PM 发了邀请链接就先点它进团队），把页面显示的安装码 粘贴回安装窗口。PM 直接发给你的安装码也行——码有时效，过期就在网页上重新复制一个。

✓

Done

You're on the team. Your AI usage shows up on the team board, and your idle AI compute can be scheduled into the team pool — nothing else to configure. 完成。你的 AI 使用情况已接入团队，空闲算力可被调度，无需再配置任何东西。

For team leads: the invite message.

Copy, paste into your team chat, attach your invite link and you're done. 给 PM / 负责人：复制下面这段发到团队群，补上你的邀请链接即可。
